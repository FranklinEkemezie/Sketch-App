Welcome to Sketch App 🎨

How-To

  • Drag the mouse to draw, hold down Ctrl to draw a horizontal line and Shift to draw a vertical line
  • The colour palette comprising of eight different colours allows you to choose the colour of the line.
  • A random background colour is used for the canvas automatically, use the colour box to customize it.
  • Use the slider to set the weight of the line: Default is 5 (medium)
  • The Clear button clears the entire screen leaving it blank
  • To erase a small portion of the drawing use the Erase button
  • Clicking the Save button downloads the sketch in png format.
  • There are rulers, horizontally and vertically to aid in making neater sketch.
  • Down to the left, where both rulers meet indicates the current unit, you can click on it to toggle between inches and centimetres.


NB: You can always click on the Colour Palette icon to display the Info Box when needed
